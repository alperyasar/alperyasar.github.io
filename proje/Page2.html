ASUS


The Asus ZenFone is a series of Android smartphones designed, marketed and produced by Asus. It was announced at the 2014 Consumer Electronic Show in Las Vegas, Nevada for the First Generation ZenFones. In addition to its Intel Atom, Qualcomm Snapdragon, and MediaTek processors and the Android operating system, ZenFone also features the Zen UI which is a user interface from Asus.
